My name is Jocko. I make websites.

A bit about me.

I live in Boston, Massachusetts with my wife and cats. My journey into tech started with a degree in journalism. As a reporter covering the local tech beat, I became fascinated with how easily a single person could get his or her voice out into the world via the internet. I began to dabble in creating websites.

I became hooked. When I built my first website, I realized I found something that gave me the freedom and versatility I was looking for in my work. Now I've made a full switch to front-end development, where I can use my organizational skills and eye for detail to write clean, elegant code.
